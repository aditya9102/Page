Parental Support Benefits

Comprehensive benefits designed to support parents in managing both family and work commitments.

Paid Paternal Leave Policy

We provide fathers with paid paternal leave, allowing them to bond with their newborns and ease into parenthood without stress.

Main Points:
  • Duration of Leave:
    • Standard leave period of 12 weeks.
    • Extended leave up to 16 weeks for special cases.
  • Eligibility Criteria:
    • Minimum 1 year of employment.
    • Both full-time and part-time employees eligible.
  • Leave Compensation:
    • 100% salary coverage for the first 8 weeks.
    • 75% salary coverage for the remaining leave.
  • Additional Support:
    • Access to counseling services during the leave.
    • Flexible return-to-work schedule after the leave ends.

Our flexible work schedules allow parents to manage both their childcare responsibilities and their professional duties with ease.

Main Points:

    • On-site daycare facilities available.
